Biography [CV]

Currently, I work as an engineer at Beijing Institute of Control and Electronic Technology, China Aerospace Science and Technology. I obtained my master's degree in engineering with major in computer science and technolgy at School of Computer, National University of Defense Technology. Prior to that, I studied computer science and technology and received my bachelor's degree from School of Computer, Xidian University. I worked with Prof. Jianfeng Ma for undergraduate research.

My research has been focused on developing machine learning algorithms for real-world problems; and the fundamental problems regarding to low-data regime issues, such as few-shot learning, semi-supervised learning and unsupervised learning.

I also spent half a year at Microsoft Research Asia, working with Beibei Shi on academic collaboration program management.
